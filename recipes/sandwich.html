Easy sand-wich recipe

ingredients :

  • Sardines
  • Butter
  • Sliced bread
  • Cayenne pepper

steps to prepare :

  1. mix filling ingredients, spread it on bread
  2. cover bread, cut off crusts and cut into desired shapes
  3. cover the sandwiches with cling film before serving so they dont dry out from exposure to air.
  4. if you wish,you can make use of the toaster!
