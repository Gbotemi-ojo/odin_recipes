fried rice delicacy

fried rice garnished with the finest of ingredients

ingredients

  • 3¾ cups (750g) long grain parboiled rice
  • Chicken (whole chicken or chicken drumsticks)
  • 100g cow liver
  • 1 tablespoon Nigerian curry powder
  • ½ cup green beans
  • 3 carrots
  • Salt (to taste)
  • 3 onions
  • 3 stock/boullion cubes
  • 1 tablespoon thyme

steps to prepare:

  1. cut to vegetables to bits and wash appopriately
  2. cook the cow liver and the whole chicken,cut to shreds then season with knorr chicken
  3. parboil the rice so all water drains out
  4. .Pour the chicken stock into a sieve to remove all traces of onions and thyme
  5. also, add 1 tablespoon of plain yellow curry powder, then add salt to taste
  6. Now, transfer the rice to a casserole dish or another pot to help cool it down quickly

    Again, this is essential to keep the grains from sticking to each other

    If left in the original hot pot, the rice will continue to cook and eventually stick together

  7. Now pour a small amount of vegetable oil into a frying pan

    When the oil is hot, add the diced onions,diced cow liver,diced veggies and the rice.

  8. Stir till all the ingredients have mixed well and transfer to a dry pot
  9. your fried rice is ready!
